Hello, I’m Ridwan Whitehead

Non-verbal + regulation of learning + CV/MLLM methods = my expertise.

Intrigued by the role of non-verbal behaviors in regulation of learning, I build tools, datasets, and workflows to make sense of multimodal interaction data in the context of regulation of learning. I am part of the excellent team at the Learning, Education, and Technology Research Lab (LET Lab) at the University of Oulu.
